Provide verification details for a person or relevant legal entity with significant control (PSC / RLE)

Use this service to provide the identity verification details for a company’s person with significant control (PSC) or relevant legal entity (RLE). This needs to be done within 14 days of the anniversary of the company’s incorporation date. For newly appointed PSCs this has to be done within 28 days of the appointment.

What is a PSC

A PSC owns or controls a company. They’re sometimes called ‘beneficial owners’. A PSC has at least one of the following:

  • more than 25% of shares in the company
  • more than 25% of voting rights in the company
  • the right to appoint or remove the majority of the board of directors

What is a RLE

A Relevant Legal Entity (RLE) is a corporate entity, like a limited liability partnership, or limited company, that exercises significant influence or control over a company, much like a PSC. In fact, an RLE would qualify as a Person with significant control (PSC) of another company if it were an individual. Essentially, it is a corporate PSC.

OR

This type of person with significant control (PSC) is a corporate body or firm that owns or controls a company. This can be determined by a number of different criteria including voting rights, rights to share in surplus assets, or the right to appoint or remove the members of the company.

Who can use this service

Every PSC in a company and the relevant officer of the RLE need to provide verification details, confirming that they have verified their ID. This is why this service is for individuals who are:

  • setting up a new company and need to link their verified identity with the PSC record
  • existing PSCs
  • linking another PSC’s verified identity with the company record
  • relevant officers of a relevant legal entity (usually a company director, or similar)

What you'll need

To complete this task for each PSC or relevant officer, you will need to:

  • verify your identity, if you haven’t already
  • have the Companies House personal code for each PSC and relevant officier of the RLE (if confirming someone else)
  • the company registration number
What is a Companies House personal code?
This is a unique code that is received once a person's identity is verified with Companies House.
