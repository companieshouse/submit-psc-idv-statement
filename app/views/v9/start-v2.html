Provide identity verification details for a person with significant control (PSC)

Use this service to provide a Companies House personal code for a person with significant control.

You can also use this service to check when a company's PSCs need to provide their personal codes.

You cannot use this service if the PSC has their personal information protected at Companies House (or is waiting for the results of an application)

When you need to provide verification details

This depends on whether the PSC was registered with Companies House before or after identity verification requirements were introduced on [date].

If they were registered as a PSC before [date], they have 14 days starting from the companyâ€™s confirmation statement date. They cannot do it before then.

If they were registered as a PSC after [date], they have 14 days starting from the date they were registered.

! Warning If a PSC does not comply with identity verification requirements, they will be committing an offence and may have to pay a financial penalty or fine.

Before you start

To use this service, you'll need:

  • to sign in, or create sign in details
  • the company number
  • the PSC's Companies House personal code
What is a Companies House personal code?

This is an 11 character code that is given to a person after they have verified their identity for Companies House.

Find out more about verifying your identity for Companies House.

It can take up to 10 minutes to provide verification details for a PSC.
