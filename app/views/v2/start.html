Confirm a person with significant control (PSC)

Use this service to identify a Person with Significant Control (PSC) in a company and confirm who they are. This needs to be done within 14 days of the anniversary of the company's incorporation date. The PSC might be you, or someone associated with the company. A company can have one or more PSCs.

What is a PSC

A PSC owns or controls a company. They’re sometimes called ‘beneficial owners’. A PSC has at least one of the following:

  • more than 25% of shares in the company
  • more than 25% of voting rights in the company
  • the right to appoint or remove the majority of the board of directors

Who can use this service

You can use this service if you are a PSC or you are confirming someone else who is a PSC. If submitting a confirmation on behalf of someone else, you will need their Companies House personal code.

What is a Companies House personal code?
This is a unique code that is received once a person's identity is verified with Companies House.

What you'll need

To complete this task, you’ll you will need to:

  • verify your identity, if you haven’t already
  • have the Companies House personal code for each PSC (if confirming someone else)

You may also need the company registration number for which you are confirming a PSC.
